Homelessness

Environmental protection

Environmental protection involves the preservation of natural resources and ecosystems through policies and sustainable practices to diminish the negative impacts of human activities on this planet

Poverty

Poverty is a widespread socioeconomic problem characterized by the lack of resources and opportunities necessary for the decent standard of living.
